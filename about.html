Something more about me.

My professional career began with being a teacher. After completing the five-year master's degree in early education, for many years I have had great joy in passing my knowledge to the youngest and developing my artistic skills. In addition to my obvious duties, I also ran creative interest clubs and workshops for children and adults, I created posters and advertising leaflets. In order to improve the services I was providing, I graduated in computer graphics design.
After some time, I decided to change my path and started working as a website coordinator. I was responsible for the correct assignment of tickets to tasks, and for communication between clients and agents. This occupation - how different from being a teacher, allowed me to observe the correlation between the quality of the delivered product and the level of user satisfaction.
Moving to California was another career switch opportunity. For three years I worked as a content reviewer checking thousands of applications before publishing them in Play Store. Then I thought again about the importance of consciously planning and designing a product before making it available to the intended user. This made me interested in UX design.
Who am I? An ambitious and fast learner. Passionate about trying new fields of art. A huge fan of sport, music, traveling, food, and my dog.
