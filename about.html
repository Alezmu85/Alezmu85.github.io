ABOUT CREATIVE UX HOME

My professional career began as a teacher. After completing my master's degree in early childhood education, I had the great joy of passing my knowledge onto the youngest and brightest people while developing my own artistic skills. In addition to my teaching duties, I organized creative interest clubs and workshops for children and adults. In order to improve and promote the services I was providing, I studied computer graphics design and began designing the posters and advertising leaflets for these programs.

After some time, I decided to change my path and started working as a customer support coordinator. In this role, I was responsible for assigning tickets to tasks, and coordinating communications between clients and agents. This occupation, while different from being a teacher, allowed me to observe the correlation between the quality of the delivered product and the level of user satisfaction.

Moving to California was another career-shifting opportunity. For three years I worked as a content reviewer checking thousands of applications before publishing them in the Google Play Store.
