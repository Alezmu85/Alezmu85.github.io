Who am I?

Being an educator by vocation has granted me many opportunities to introduce others to a world of creative expression – whether through teaching or through works of my own. As a graphic designer, I actively apply my artistic energies to meaningful projects of all types of mediums. As a customer support coordinator, I understand the importance of the users' expectations when articulating meaningful experiences. As an app reviewer, I was able to appreciate the efforts that developers put into delivering valuable products. And, as my career evolves to add UX design to my skill set, I’m able to combine and apply all these experiences together.
